Steps to connect components

  1. Connect a dc motor to jetson nano using L298 motor driver.
  2. Pin 32 & 33 are for PWM output and rest pins are for L298N inputs.


  3. Direct connection of servo motor with jetson nano is not possible. Use servo motor driver PCA9685.
  4. VCC of PCA9685 will be connected to pin 1 of j41 of jetson nano.
  5. GND will be connected to pin 6 of j41.
  6. SDA will be connected to pin 27 of j41.
  7. SCL will be connected to pin 28 of j41.
